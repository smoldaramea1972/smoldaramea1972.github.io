When you subscribe to Premium via the iOS app, the billing is handled by Apple and managed through iTunes.
To cancel the auto-renewal for your subscription, please do the following:

  2. Tap on iTunes & App Stores
  3. Tap on your Apple ID at the top
  4. View Apple ID
  5. Tap the Manage button under Subscriptions
